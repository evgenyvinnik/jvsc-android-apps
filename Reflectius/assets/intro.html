Features

 Day 1 features:
  • To create a new stylish 24 clock widget, long-press in Home and select Widget > 24 Clock.
    • You can then configure widget specific options before installing your widget.
  • Use the "24" icon in the all-apps drawer to configure global settings such as hourly chime or global mute.
  • Hourly chime and other sounds will not play when your device is muted, in vibrate-only or during a phone call.
  • Battery saver: clock stops updating when the screen is off or after 30 seconds when you're not in the Home app.

Known Issues
  • The 24 Clock widget has been tested with the default Android Home app. It may not work properly with alternate home apps.
  • If you use a custom home app and the clock never updates in home, try to uncheck "Detect Home is Active" in the global options.
